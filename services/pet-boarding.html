Waggy Tails - Pet Boarding

At Waggy Tails Pet Clinic in Chennai, we understand that leaving your pet behind while you're away can be difficult and stressful. That's why we offer safe and comfortable pet boarding services for your furry friends.

Our state-of-the-art facility is staffed with experienced and compassionate professionals who are dedicated to providing the best possible care for your pet. We offer a variety of boarding options to suit your pet's individual needs, including private rooms, shared rooms, and spacious indoor and outdoor play areas.

We understand that each pet is unique and has their own individual needs and preferences. That's why we work closely with you to create a personalized plan that fits your pet's needs. We provide regular exercise, playtime, and individual attention to ensure that your pet is happy and comfortable while they are in our care.

We also provide education to our clients on proper care, nutrition, and preventive measures that can be taken to ensure their pet's long-term health. We are available 24/7, so you can reach us at any time in case of emergency.
