Waggy Tails - Pet Dental Care

At Waggy Tails Pet Clinic in Chennai, we understand the importance of dental care for your pet's overall health and well-being. Our team of experienced veterinarians and staff, including best pet dentists, are dedicated to providing the highest level of care for your furry friends.

We offer comprehensive dental care services including routine teeth cleaning, extractions, and oral surgery. Our state-of-the-art facilities are equipped with the latest technology, which allows us to provide the most advanced care for your pet. We also have a fully-stocked pharmacy and in-house laboratory for fast and accurate diagnosis and treatment.

We understand that your pet's dental health is an important part of their overall health, and that's why we provide personalized care for your pet, and work closely with you to create a dental care plan that fits your pet's individual needs. We also provide education to our clients on proper care, nutrition, and preventive measures that can be taken to ensure their pet's long-term dental health.

Our goal is to help keep your pet healthy and happy, so that they can be a part of your family for many years to come. We are available 24/7, so you can reach us at any time in case of emergency.
